# APEX Hashtag Generator

AI-powered tool for generating trending and relevant Instagram hashtags to boost your posts' reach and engagement. Simply enter your post idea, and let AI do the work.

## Features

*   **AI-Driven:** Generates hashtags based on your input.
*   **Easy to Use:** Simple interface for quick hashtag generation.
*   **Optimized for Growth:** Helps you discover relevant and trending hashtags.

## How to Use

1.  Enter a description of your Instagram post in the input field.
2.  Click 'Generate Hashtags'.
3.  Copy the generated hashtags and paste them into your Instagram post.
